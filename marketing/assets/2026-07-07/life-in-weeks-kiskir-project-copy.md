## Positioning

Life in Weeks by KISKIR is a static public life-grid tool for mapping personal timelines and public-figure profiles one week at a time.

## Short Description

Life in Weeks turns biographies into a 100-year week grid. Exact events pin to week cells, approximate public facts stay visibly approximate, and the exported image remains useful outside the app.

## Longer Description

Life in Weeks is a KISKIR project for seeing a whole life as a visible timeline. The page supports personal timelines, public-figure profiles, sourced life events, stage color bands, exportable images, and a prompt workflow for building new profile data.

The product's strongest boundary is honesty about time. Exact dates can land on week cells. Year-only, month-only, or age-level facts render as rails or anchors instead of being forced into fake precision.

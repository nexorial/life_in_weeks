# Cristiano Ronaldo

## Status

Curated news profile for 2026-07-10.

## Story Line

> Madeira to Dallas: six World Cups, twenty years, and the final square of a tournament career.

## Why This Is in the News

On 2026-07-05, Cristiano Ronaldo confirmed that his sixth World Cup would be his last. One day later, he played
all 104 minutes as Portugal lost 1–0 to Spain in the round of 16, ending a World Cup run that began in 2006.

The final match followed two fresh records: on 2026-06-23 he became the first man to score at six World Cups, and
on 2026-07-02 he scored his first World Cup knockout-stage goal at age 41. The profile says only that his World Cup
career has ended; he had not announced retirement from Portugal or professional football as of 2026-07-10.
